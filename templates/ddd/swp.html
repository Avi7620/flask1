Simulation Plus - Drug Discovery and Development Software

Simulations Plus Inc (Simulations Plus) develops and markets software for drug discovery and development for the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. The company develops absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion, and toxicity modeling and simulation software.
