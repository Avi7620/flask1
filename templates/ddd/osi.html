OSIRIS - Chemical Toxicity Prediction Tool

OSIRIS is a software tool used for predicting the potential toxicity of chemical compounds. It analyzes the chemical structure of a compound and provides information on its potential hazards, such as skin irritation, eye damage, or mutagenicity. OSIRIS helps researchers and regulatory agencies evaluate the safety of chemicals and make informed decisions about their use in various applications, from pharmaceuticals to industrial chemicals. It's like a virtual safety tester for chemicals, helping to protect human health and the environment.
